SapplyValues

What is SapplyValues?

SapplyValues is combines the questions of the Sapply political compass test with the UI of 9Axes, which is in turn based on 8values. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be displayed on a political compass.

There are questions in the test.
